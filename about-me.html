About me

Who am I?

I'm a product manager and product designer with 10+ years of experience designing, developing, and launching products to meet users' wants and needs. Most recently, I was Director of Product Management at Pegasus Solutions, where we were working on ambitious call center applications, hotelier tools, and consumer booking engines.

With COVID-19's impact on travel tech, my core projects have been put on pause and I'm furloughed. I'm looking for opportunities in the next few months.

Background

How I got into design: I originally got into design during undergrad at Stanford, when I discovered there was a major that would combine my love of visual arts and math. I ended up pursuing a B.S. in Product Design. (Previously, I'd had no idea what I would do after spending my junior year of high school at a pre-professional fine arts school, and my senior year as president of the math team.)

Working history: I've worked across departments, in roles that required some understanding of users in order to design for them, from market research and advertising, to customer operations, to UX and more. With this breadth of experience, I bring the most value to strategic product management roles, but am also interested in other consumer experience opportunities.
