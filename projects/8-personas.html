Defining personas at a foreign language school


Summary

The challenge: Define student personas for an adult foreign language school

What I did: Survey & quantitative survey analysis, 15 user interviews & analysis, personas, product recommendations

The result: 4 personas**

Duration: 2 months

When: Fall 2014

**For confidentiality reasons, I'm unable to publish the results, including the final personas, but I'd like to walk you through my process.



Design Process

Step 1: Survey

First, I designed and distributed a survey to Greater NYC residents. This survey would double as a recruitement survey and a way to get some qualitative data about what New Yorkers do in their spare time and what sort of after-work learning experiences they would like.



Step 2: Survey analysis & recruitment

I analyzed about ~350 valid survey replies to get some preliminary data that would guide my focus in the user interviews. I started to recruit participants for the next step based on age, gender, educational background, pasttimes, experience with the client's school, and more.

Step 3: User interviews & analysis

15 user interviews were conducted, followed by an exciting week of re-watching videos, jotting notes on post-its, and looking for patterns.
